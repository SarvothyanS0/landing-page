About

Say hello to iPhone 14. Big leaps in safety, battery life, and camera quality. The iPhone 14 was released on September 16, 2022 and features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display, powered by the Apple A15 Bionic chip. It has a dual-camera system with 12MP wide and ultrawide lenses, offering sensor-shift optical image stabilization. The device supports 5G connectivity, Face ID, and MagSafe wireless charging.
